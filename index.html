Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, therapy, and mental well-being.

How do I book a therapy session? +

You can book a therapy session through our online portal by selecting a therapist and available time slot that works for you.

Are online sessions available? +

Yes, we offer both in-person and online therapy sessions to accommodate your preferences and needs.

What's the difference between therapy and coaching? +

Therapy focuses on treating mental health conditions and emotional difficulties, while coaching is more focused on goal-setting and personal development.
